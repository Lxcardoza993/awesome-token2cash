## 9. 🧩 Absorbable Ideas Cheatsheet

- **Multi-agent debate / role specialization** — Split analysis into specialized personas (bull, bear, risk manager, portfolio manager) and resolve disagreements via structured debate. (exemplified by: TradingAgents, orallexa-ai-trading-agent, ai-hedge-fund)
- **Tool-augmented retrieval over filings / RAG for finance** — Ground LLM outputs in SEC filings, earnings transcripts, and market data via retrieval augmentation. (exemplified by: FinRAG, openbb-rag-financial-research-agent, multi-horizon-financial-agent)
- **LLM-driven sentiment-to-signal pipeline** — Transform news, social media, or earnings-call sentiment into quantitative trading signals. (exemplified by: twitter-alpha-sentiment-tracker-v2, FinNLP, ProsusAI/finBERT)
- **LoRA/PEFT fine-tuning recipe on financial corpus** — Adapt general LLMs to finance via lightweight fine-tuning on earnings calls, news, and reports. (exemplified by: FinGPT, FinLoRA, PIXIU)
- **Memory-augmented trading agent** — Layer short-term and long-term memory into an agent to persist portfolio context and adapt behavior. (exemplified by: FinMem-LLM-StockTrading)
- **ReAct + tool-use for research report generation** — Use an LLM with reasoning and action loops to gather data and write investment research reports. (exemplified by: deep-research-machine, stock_research_agent)
- **Backtesting + paper-trading sandbox** — Validate agent strategies with historical market replay and broker paper accounts before live deployment. (exemplified by: FinRL, Qbot, AlpacaTradingAgent)
- **Risk-guard / human-in-the-loop veto** — Insert guardrails or human approval gates between signal generation and trade execution. (exemplified by: AutoTrader-AgentEdge, ai-hedge-fund, LLM-TradeBot)
- **Multi-modal market understanding** — Combine price charts, alternative data, and text to improve stock analysis. (exemplified by: StockerLens, FinAgent)
- **Market simulation with generative agents** — Simulate economic/trading populations with LLM agents to study emergent market dynamics. (exemplified by: TwinMarket, LLM-Economist, ai-economist)
- **Financial benchmark-driven development** — Use finance-specific QA, embedding, and trading benchmarks to evaluate LLM/agent capabilities. (exemplified by: FinEval, financebench, BizFinBench)
